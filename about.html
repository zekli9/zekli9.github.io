About Zekli Store

Anime-inspired store created for students & anime lovers.

Who We Are

We are a small anime-focused store bringing affordable, aesthetic anime merch like figures, keychains, rings, bracelets, chains, stickers & more. Our main goal is to give middle-school and high-school students access to cool anime accessories without huge prices.

What We Do

We focus on trending anime series, fan-favourite characters, and aesthetic accessories that match school life, casual outfits, gaming setups & desk decor.
